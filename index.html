Falling in love with Project Scarlett

Here is an attempt to persuade myself and other gamers to fall in love with the up and coming Xbox Series X despite its Tower life design.
I must say they have finally lived up to its name exBOX.
Xbox project Scarlett is predicted to lead the frontier of the next gen gaming sphere. So what does this mean? Basically means it is set out to thrash every other gaming console currently released as of yet, but for now we shall reserve our comments on the PS5 release and its specs as they are yet to be announced.

To substantiate this we shall look at the specs of the Xbox Series X and why me and every gamer should consider making a purchase.

  • 4K/60fps

    • The more frames per second the smoother the quality, we are guaranteed an out of this world gaming experience given by the Zen 2 and RDNA architecture from AMD.

  • NVMe solid-state storage

    • Just know that it is faster than the SATA SSD.

To sum it all, the xbox Series X will give a faster better performance with less power consumption than its current competitors providing an exceptional ideal dream standard of every gamers wish.

So Price wise Chief?
- It is said to range between £500-£600, miss me I am booking a flight back home.
